Overview

The study of the Moduli spaces of sheaves has important implications in different areas of mathematics, e.g. in enumerative geometry. In this reading seminar we will explore the existence as schemes of Moduli spaces of sheaves on K3 surfaces and their geometry. The seminar is mostly based on the book Geometry of Moduli spaces of sheaves by Huybrechts and Lehn. The prerrequisites for this seminar are the courses Algebraic Geometry I and II as taught in Bonn, or equivalently the second and third chapters of Hartshorne's book.
