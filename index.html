Alejandro Ovalle

I am interested in enumerative algebraic geometry, moduli problems, derived categories, and the interplay between these areas.

In Septermber 2025 I finished writing my master's thesis on the enumerative geometry of K3 surfaces at the University of Bonn under the supervision of Prof. Richard Thomas and Prof. Daniel Huybrechts. In my thesis, I explored BPS invariants on K3s and their relation to stable pair invariants.

In November 2025, I will begin a research internship at the Max Planck Institute (MiP) to explore the interplay between enumerative algebraic geometry and tropical geometry.
