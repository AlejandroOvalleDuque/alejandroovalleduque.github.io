Alejandro Ovalle

I am interested in enumerative algebraic geometry, moduli problems, derived categories, and the interplay between these areas.

In Mai 2026, I will start a PhD at the University of Hannover, in which I will explore logarithmic algebraic geometry and its role in enumerative geometry.

Before that, I completed my master's degree at the University of Bonn, where I studied BPS and stable pair invariants on K3 surfaces, and I did a research internship at the Max Planck Institute (MiS) on tropical curve counting.
